About Me

Hello!

Thank you for visiting my page ✨ My name is Monia and I'm a graphic designer, illustrator, freelance artist and translator.

I love creating collages, drawings and paintings using both digital and traditional media. Japanese and Korean cultures have a strong influence on my work and artistic interests.

I mostly work with Adobe Photoshop and Illustrator, but I also experiment with video formats in Premiere Pro. Traditional tools I enjoy include oil and acrylic paints, pencils, markers and photography.

Combining techniques and experimenting with different media is at the heart of everything I create.
